Hackathon


A coding challenge of 12 hours will be conducted on 5th September, 2019 at Mahindra Ecole Centrale.

Guidelines
  • Maximum team size: 3
  • This challenge is open to all the undergraduate students irrespective of their branch of study
  • The interested students have to register before 15th August, 2019
  • The theme of the hackathon will be given by the organisers just before the hackathon starts
  • The top 2 teams will win prizes

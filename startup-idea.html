Startup Idea


URS 2019 is giving you a chance to pitch your creative start-up ideas with our Entrepreneur and Incubation cell (EIC) at Mahindra Ecole Centrale on 6th September, 2019

Guidelines
  • This competition is open to all the undergraduate students with start-up ideas in any area
  • The interested students have to register before 15th August, 2019
  • The students will be expected to give a short talk, explaining their start-up idea
  • Students can participate as individuals or as a team (upto 4 members)
  • The best 3 start-up ideas will be awarded prizes

Undergraduate Research Symposium

Mahindra École Centrale is organising its second annual R&D showcase URS@MEC 2019. Students from various institutes will showcase their projects and research activities in the form of posters, talks and design prototypes. Various technical events like hackathon and start-up idea challenges are scheduled during this symposium. Eminent personalities from Academia and Industry will give keynote lectures along with student presentations. Representatives from Industry and Academia will participate, interact and evaluate various technical sessions along with faculty of MÉC.

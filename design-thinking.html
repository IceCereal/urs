Design Thinking


Display any technical prototype/design/application during URS 2019

Guidelines
  • This competition is about displaying any technical prototype or design or application during our technical fest URS-2019
  • The competition will be held on 6th September, 2019
  • Students can participate as individuals or as a team (upto 4 members)
  • The interested students have to register before 20th August, 2019
  • A short abstract of not more than 300 words must be submitted at the time of registration
  • The interested students should make sure their work is complete and any damage in the prototype will not be encouraged
  • The students participating must be able to talk about the motivation behind their prototypes, the process involved in making it and its applications
  • The top 3 prototypes will be awarded prizes

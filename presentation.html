Undergraduate Research Symposium

Mahindra Ecole Centrale is organising its second annual R&D showcase URS@MEC 2019. Students from various institutes will showcase their projects and research activities in the form of posters, talks and design prototypes. Various technical events like hackathon and start-up idea challenges are scheduled during this symposium. Eminent personalities from Academia and Industry will give keynote lectures along with student presentations. Representatives from Industry and Academia will participate, interact and evaluate various technical sessions along with faculty of MEC.
